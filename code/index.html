FAQ

Sounds like fun! Can I try?

Of course! You are more than welcome to contact us and sign up for a free, but sweaty workout!


Do I have to be really fit to keep up?

No, not allt all! All of our WOD's (Workout Of the Day) have different levels, so everyone can participate to the best of their ability.


Do I need some special eqipment??

No, your usual workout clothes and shoes are fine!


What is a"box"?

The Box = a crossfit gym. It's called a box because it's typically in a warehouse-type space. Instead of seeing the machines you see in a typical gym, you're more likely to see stacks of weights, barbells, pull-up bars and lots of open space.
